Smiles & Frowns is a true mom and pop story. Tinkering with our own rewards chart at home led us to an approach we started calling our "5 Cs of Positive Reinforcement™": Clarity, Consistency, Collaboration, Consequence and a game-like sense of Challenge. And that led to some great features you won't find anywhere else, like:
  • Great premade boards that are well thought out and easy to customize
  • A simple formula that lets kids create their own rewards-buying power
  • A simple way to set rewards based on Money, Time, Treats or Goals
  • The ability to invite spouses and other guardians to join your board
  • An easy way to review performance and even export a PDF of results
  • Automatic synching between devices
